Dream Vacation: Tokyo, Japan

There is only one place that I have on my bucket list, one place that I wish to see before I leave this plane of existence. Tokyo, Japan. This is a webpage dedicated to the place I've been wanting to visit ever since I was 12 years old, so scroll down and enjoy!
Tokyo is the capital of Japan, and the distance between San Antonio, Tx and Tokyo, Japan is 6,559 miles!

What I'm excited to see and do:

Although there a lot of Ramen shops in San Antonio, I'm sure that there will a significant difference from the authenic ramen shops in Tokyo. I can't wait to try them all!!!
Based off the pictures I see online, Japan seems SO different from anything I would see here in the United States. The parks, the mountains, even the nature. It all seems so different and wonderful.
Did you know: As of 2022, Japan is home to more than 32,000 ramen shops.
One place I will for sure go for a full day is Universal Japan: Nintendo Land.
There aren't good arcades left when I live, and I hear the ones in Japan are way different from the ones the United States. I would to go arcade hopping and try as many as I can.
